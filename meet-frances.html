Meet Frances

Mother, Wife, North Carolinian.

Frances grew up in rural North Carolina. She knows our values, she knows what makes our communities great. As a mother she has a first hand experience at what our students are facing in today's schools. Active-shooter drills, curriculum being taken away, books being banned, all things that are weakening our public school system.

As a wife to a Marine veteran, she understands that our state needs to do better support our veterans. They fought for our country and our rights, and we need to fight for them once we get home.

Most of all, she is a proud North Carolinian. She understands the value of hard work and what coming together to fight for common sense solutions to our every day issues.

I'm not a career politician. We need grassroots energy to flip this district.

I need you.

Running for office is not something I had in mind. But, we are at a cross roads in North Carolina. Decency and common sense approaches to tackling our kitchen table issues are what is going to bring progress to rural North Carolina. I get it, times are tough. All I'm asking for is $5, $10, or whatever you can spare to help us fight for what is right. I'm not accepting any corporate PAC money, this campaign will be funded by the people who send me to Raleigh.
